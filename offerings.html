Tithe & Offerings

Why We Give

Giving is an act of worship and obedience. As believers, we recognize that everything belongs to God. Our tithes and offerings support the ministry, missions, community outreach, and the spiritual growth of our congregation.

Tithe (10%)

The tithe is a tenth of your income given to God through the local church. It is a covenant practice that honors God and supports the work of the ministry.

Offerings

Beyond the tithe, offerings are voluntary gifts given for special projects, missions, building fund, and community outreach initiatives.
