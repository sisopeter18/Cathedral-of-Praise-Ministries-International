About the Church

Cathedral of Praise Ministries International is a Christ-centered church located in Imara Daima, Embakasi South. We exist to preach the Gospel, build believers, and impact communities through worship, discipleship, and missions.

Our Mission

To preach the Gospel of Jesus Christ, build a generation of believers rooted in God's Word, and impact our communities through worship, discipleship, and missions.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of hope and transformation in Embakasi South and beyond, where believers are empowered to impact their sphere of influence for Christ.
